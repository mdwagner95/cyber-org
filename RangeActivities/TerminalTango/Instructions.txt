The Dance:
Pascal is looking forward to attending 
the school dance with her friends. They 
have been making plans, and Pascal 
doesn’t want to miss out on anything. 
Help Pascal with her attire, the 
restruant and flowers. 

Attire:
Navigate to the attire folder. There 
are 6 various styles of attire to 
choose from. Delete the 5 styles of 
attire you would not want.

Restaurant:
Create a list of places to eat. 

Flowers:
There are 3 flower options. Find 
the correct flowers and delete 
the others.
